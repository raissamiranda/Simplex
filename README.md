# Simplex

Simplex algorithm is used to solve the Linear Programming (LP) optimization problems.

## Description

This project solves linear problems in canonical form:
> max $c^Tx$
> 
> subject to $Ax \le b$



$$
A = \left[
\begin{array}{cccc}
a_{1,1} & a_{1,2} & ... & a_{1,m} \\
a_{2,1} & a_{2,2} & ... & a_{2,m} \\
... & ... & ... & ... \\
a_{n,1} & a_{n,2} & ... & a_{n,m} \Box
\end{array}
\right]
$$
